    • Research Interests

    Ocean Biogeochemistry
  • Understanding the distributions of trace elements and isotopes (Nd isotopes, REEs, Fe, redox sensitive metals etc.) in the ocean, focusing on the role of sedimentary sources and early diagenesis
    • Plio-Pleistocene Paleoceanography,
  • Reconstructing past ocean circulation and its relation to the climate system using sediment archives (foraminiferal proxies, authigenic and lithogenic sediment geochemistry)
