Research Interests

Ocean bigeochemistry, understanding the distributions of trace elements and isotopes (Nd isotopes, REEs, Fe, redox sensitive metals etc.) in the ocean, focusing on the role of sedimentary sources and early diagenesis

Plio-Pleistocene Paleoceanography, reconstructing past ocean circulation and its relation to the climate system using sediment archives (foraminiferal proxies, authigenic and lithogenic sediment geochemistry)
